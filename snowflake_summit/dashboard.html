How the scores work

Every partner is rated 0–10 on five directional, scouting-lens dimensions, then blended into an Overall:

  • Bryan Recommend — career & networking fit for Bryan specifically; baseline calibrated from a sample of 30 vendor booth selling pitches (directional — to confirm).
  • Snowflake — relevance to the Snowflake ecosystem (native apps, integrations, Summit presence, joint go-to-market).
  • AI — strength & relevance of the vendor's AI / ML / agent story.
  • Retail / Customer — fit for retail & customer-analytics use cases (the scouting focus).
  • IPO / Upside — growth trajectory, funding / valuation momentum, and exit / investment upside.
  • Overall — the simple mean (average) of the five scores.

Scores are Bryan's directional ratings from the scouting workbook (some researched, some template / estimated). Tier is a curated priority call (A = must-see) that uses Overall as a guideline (≈ 7.5+ / 6+ / below), set editorially. Funding & valuation figures were enriched via AI web research — verify before relying.
