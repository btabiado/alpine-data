How to read this dashboard

A cheat sheet you can come back to.

Big swings matter
A jump like +30 → −5 overnight is a real regime shift — click any signal above to see what drove it.
Bands > absolute number
"Moved from Constructive to Monitor" is a real signal. "Still in Monitor" is mostly noise — bands hop at the edges.
Disagreement is interesting
When the macro signal goes one way and pillar averages go the other, that’s where the active read lives. Look for green/red mixed in step 2.
13F lags by a quarter
Big institutional positions are reported with a ~45-day delay. Insider buys are fresher (within days) but can be noisy around earnings.
"as of" = snapshot time
The page doesn’t auto-update. The number is fixed until the next 23:00 UTC run. If the date looks old, the pipeline is probably stuck — check the Health page.
What this is NOT. Not a trading signal. Not investment advice. Not a forecast of next week’s prices. It’s a daily directional read on committed public data — insiders, 13F filers, fundamentals, macro. Do your own work before risking real money.
