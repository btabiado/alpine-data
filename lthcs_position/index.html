How sizing works

  1. Band multiplier — Elite 1.5×, High Confidence 1.2×, Constructive 1.0×, Monitor 0.7×, Weakening 0.3×, Review 0× (excluded).
  2. Raw weight = composite × band multiplier, normalised so eligible tickers sum to 100%.
  3. Per-position cap — Conservative 5%, Balanced 8%, Aggressive 12%. Override the cap manually if you want a tighter or looser bound.
  4. Overflow from clipped positions redistributes proportionally to remaining sub-cap positions until convergence.
  5. Review-band tickers and zero-score rows are skipped — their bankroll stays in the cash bucket unless the rest of the universe can absorb it.
