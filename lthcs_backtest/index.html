Sample size disclaimer

90 calendar days = 63 trading days. IC estimates have a standard error of ~±0.04. Numbers in the noise range (|x| < 0.04) are NOT distinguishable from zero. Treat the first 30 days post-backfill as “building confidence in report shape”; 4+ months of history is when these numbers become statistically credible.
