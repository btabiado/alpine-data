Three modes: By ticker shows a full composite-score trajectory + every band-change event for one symbol. By band lists tickers that occupied a band at any point in a window. By streak ranks the longest consecutive runs in each band across the universe.

Computes the top 10 longest consecutive same-band runs across every ticker's full history, grouped by band. Loads all 178 history files (lazy, in parallel; 5 s cutoff — whatever is in by then is ranked).
